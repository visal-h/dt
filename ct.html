Importing the CT Token to MetaMask

To import the CT token to MetaMask, follow these steps:

  1. Open MetaMask and log in to your account.
  2. Click on the "Assets" tab.
  3. Click on "Add Token" and select "Custom Token".
  4. Enter the following details:
    • Address: 0xB31682b0Ee0c4C4faAADE9b93432DC406D29b8ae
    • Symbol: CT
    • Decimals: 18
  5. Click "Next" and then "Add Tokens" to complete the import.

Now you have successfully imported the CT token to your MetaMask wallet.
